Day of The African Child

Over 300 participants turned up to celebrate the Day of the African Child through the power of sport. Beyond the court, children engaged in comprehensive SRHR education, environmental stewardship, and youth development dialogues proving that basketball can be more than a game. From HPV vaccinations to health check-ups, the event also offered vital services, showing that sports for social impact is not only scalable but transformative. This was just the beginning and a very successful one. Let the children play. Let them learn. Let them lead. Thank you so much everyone who made this a success and a great day for the kids, let us continue creating opportunities and leveraging on sports as a catalyst for growth! The children are the future.
